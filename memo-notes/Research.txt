Text Summarization Technologies have a significant impact on the comprehension level of students with ADHD. Emphasizing the 
solutions and making use of the identified challenges that ADHD students are experiencing. We, Researchers, Identified the significant effects of using summarization technology.

Enhanced Focus, Applying the techniques to minimize the noise level of the text content. ADHD students are able to learn and extract the information from the text content without losing focus.

Reduced Cognitive Load, ADHD students who suffer from processing and organizing information can now understand and digest the information from the summarized text content.
